performance.create_drawdowns(pnl)¶

Calculate the largest peak-to-trough drawdown of the PnL curve as well as the duration of the drawdown.

Parameters:pnl – Series; period percentage returns.
Returns:Series; drawdown. float; max of drawdown. int; max of drawdown duration.
performance.create_sharpe_ratio(returns, periods=252)¶

Create the Sharpe ratio for the strategy, based on a benchmark of zero (i.e. no risk-free rate information).

Parameters:
  • returns – Series; period percentage returns.
  • periods – int; Daily (252), Hourly (252*6.5), Minutely(252*6.5*60) etc.
Returns:

float; Sharpe ratio
